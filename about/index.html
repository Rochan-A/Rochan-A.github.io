About Me

Fun fact, most people can’t pronounce my first name. It’s roh-chan, not raw-chan or roh-shan. I’m a 19 year old, read a lot on various (sometimes random) topics, knew what I wanted to do at the age of 13, I can’t sleep after 7am and play almost every sport. I spent the first 17 years of my life in Bangalore, India. It’s only recently that I spend majority of the year in Hyderabad, India where I am studying undergraduate Computer Science at Mahindra Ecole Centrale.

I’ve always had a liking towards computers in general. It started of with robotics and gradually progressed on from there. I love research, something about exploring uncharted territories just enthralls me. As much as I like programming (I’m fluent in C, C++, golang, python & R), I believe it’s just a tool. It’s the underlying algorithms and theory that really kindle my interest.
